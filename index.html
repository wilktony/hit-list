About Us

Meet HITLIST

Hitlist make noise. Lots of it.

We bring the party to Leeds' music scene, whether it's our favourite Hyde Park basement or your parent's wedding anniversary. Hitlist are bold, incisive, angry, weird, silly, romantic, and loud as f*ck.

You can't help but dance.

Chaos-wielding, the band thrives off unpredictability, reflected in our influences as diverse as Elastica to Talking Heads, Vulfpeck to Red Hot Chili Peppers. Each song is a unique flavour, a setlist pick-n-mix. Blues, funk, punk, oh my! But we won't spoil it all. You'll have to come see for yourself.
